About SapaSpots

Connecting hungry students with their favorite campus food vendors since 2025

Our Story

SapaSpots was founded in January 2025 by a group of entrepreneurial students from Makerere Business School who recognized a common challenge on campus: finding and ordering quality food quickly between classes. The name "SapaSpots" comes from "Sapa," a slang term for hunger, and "Spots," representing the various food locations around campus.

What began as a simple idea in a dorm room has quickly grown into the campus's most loved food ordering platform. Our mission is to create a seamless connection between hungry students and the diverse food vendors that serve our campus community.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize the campus dining experience by connecting students with local food vendors through an intuitive, efficient platform that saves time and reduces stress during busy academic days.

Our Vision

To be the leading food ordering platform for university campuses across East Africa, creating a vibrant ecosystem that supports local vendors while providing students with convenient access to diverse, quality food options.
